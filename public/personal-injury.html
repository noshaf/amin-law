Vehicle Accidents

Car accidents, motorcycle accidents, trucks, buses, airplanes, and watercraft. If you’ve been injured by or while operating any type of vehicle we can help you obtain monetary compensation for your injury.

Our firm has helped individuals get the money they deserve. Our lawyers are available 24/7 to talk to you about a prospective case. Our initial consultation is always free and our lawyers will work with you on a contingency basis.

The chances of you or someone you know being involved in an auto accident is extremely high. As the statistics show, if you are involved in an accident, it is very likely that you will be injured. There are number of ways you can protect yourself.

First, be a cautious driver and follow the laws. Violating the California Vehicle Code will increase your chances of being in an auto accident and will be a factor considered in any lawsuit that may follow.

Second, always make sure your vehicle is properly insured. It is estimated that 25% of drivers on the road are uninsured or under-insured. Having your vehicle properly insured is not only the law in California, it is critical for the protection of you and your family in the event of an accident.

When you are involved in an auto accident in California, the law allows you to recover damages to compensate you for a wide range of losses under the theory of negligence. Determining whether a driver was negligent is a question of fact. Each auto accident is different and requires a thorough review of the specific facts and circumstances to determine if a driver was negligent. This is something that we will analyze!

Other Accidents We also cover accidents including slip & fall, Sports/ Recreation, Pedestrian, and swimming accidents.

Injuries from a slip and fall accident can range from minor superficial injuries to serious trauma. Simple injuries such as sprains, bruises and pulled muscles can still be painful and require medical care, especially for elderly people. More serious slip and fall injuries such as broken bones, back injuries, head trauma and neck injuries always require immediate medical attention. Some injuries from slip and fall accidents may not be obvious right away and symptoms may develop or worsen over time. If you have been injured in a slip and fall accident, it is important to seek medical attention as soon as possible.

Injuries can happen in any type of sporting or recreational activity including horseback riding, swimming, boating, biking, golf, softball, football, skiing, motorsports, and many others. Certain minor injuries are common and even expected in these activities. However, if an accident is caused by someone else’s Negligence or by a defective product, you may be able to pursue a claim for damages.

Lawsuits are often brought against sports equipment manufacturers, property owners, event organizers and reckless individuals for injuries suffered while participating in sports or recreational activities.

If liability can be established, you may be able receive compensation for your past and future medical expenses, lost wages, and pain and suffering. It is important to note that personal injury claims in California are subject to a Statutes Of Limitations which limits the amount of time you have to file a lawsuit.

If you or someone close to you has been injured in any of these accidents don't hesitate to call us today for a free consultation.
